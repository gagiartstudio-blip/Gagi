Overview

Econiq is a conceptual sustainability brand built to make ethical commerce feel premium and modern. The project explores identity, messaging, and retail experiences without defaulting to common green tropes.

Slide placeholder: Brand overview

Insert slides for concept, positioning, and brand narrative.

Challenge

The main challenge was balancing premium commerce appeal with authentic sustainability messaging. The objective was to craft a brand that felt credible, elevated, and contemporary without relying on recycled or literal environmental visuals.

"The goal was to shift from 'recycled' cues toward a resilient, performance-led sustainability narrative."
  • Position the brand as both aspirational and responsible.
  • Create a visual system that works across packaging, web, and social media.
  • Ensure the identity supports premium product storytelling.

Approach

We combined audience insight, category analysis, and visual exploration to develop a flexible identity system. The approach prioritised clarity, contrast, and an elevated tone that can scale across products and campaign touchpoints.

Strategic Framework

Built a messaging framework that supports premium sustainability claims.

Visual Identity

Designed a distinctive color, typography, and iconography system for digital and packaging applications.

Solution

The final identity combines confident typography, premium color transitions, and simple visual systems that carry across product, digital, and retail experiences. The outcome is a brand that feels modern, trustworthy, and market-ready.
