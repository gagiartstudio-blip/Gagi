Overview

Challenge

The challenge was to make dense educational material feel accessible while preserving nuance and specificity, especially for an audience under time pressure.

"The primary content was dense and non-linear, so the editorial system had to keep key details visible at a glance."
  • Create a consistent information hierarchy for print and digital.
  • Design a modular layout that supports frequent scanning.
  • Keep the tone reassuring, not overwhelming.
