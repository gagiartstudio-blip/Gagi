Overview

A mental wellness product concept built around low-effort emotional support. The project focuses on simple interaction paths, calm content, and clear handoffs to deeper help.

  • Designed for users needing immediate emotional relief.
  • Prioritised trust, clarity, and low cognitive load.
  • Built a concise interaction model for daily resilience.

    Challenge

    The main challenge was designing support that feels calm and immediate, without overwhelming users with options or clinical language during sensitive moments.

    "How do we make an app feel like a deep breath rather than a new task?"
    • Reduce decision fatigue during emotional distress.
    • Keep interactions simple and reassuring.
    • Maintain companionable tone across screens.

    Approach

    We mapped user journeys, built personas, and created a low-stimulus information architecture to guide users toward support in a few gentle steps.

    Grounded the product in real user pain points and coping needs.
